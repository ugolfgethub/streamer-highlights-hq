_Last updated: April 2026_

## Scope

This software is an internal-use automation tool maintained by a single developer for the sole purpose of programmatically publishing original or properly-licensed video content to the developer's own creator accounts on TikTok (@stream_highlightshq) and Instagram (@stream_highlightshq).

## Acceptable use

The software:

1. Acts only on accounts owned and operated by the developer.
2. Publishes content the developer has the legal right to publish, including content licensed for redistribution via active Whop creator-rewards campaigns the developer is participating in.
3. Honors all platform rate limits and content-policy rules, including but not limited to TikTok's Community Guidelines and Instagram's Community Standards.
4. Does not scrape, harvest, or store data about other users.
5. Does not impersonate any person or organization.
