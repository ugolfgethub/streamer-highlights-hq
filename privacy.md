# Privacy Policy — Streamer Highlights HQ

_Last updated: April 26, 2026_

## Who we are

This privacy policy applies to the **Streamer Highlights HQ Bot**, an internal automation tool operated by Sam Krause (krausesam30@gmail.com) that publishes gaming highlight short-form videos to a single TikTok account (@stream_highlightshq) and a single Instagram account (@stream_highlightshq), both of which are owned and operated by the developer.

## What data we collect

The application collects only the data strictly necessary to publish content to the developer's own creator accounts:

- **OAuth tokens** issued to the developer by TikTok and Meta during the one-time authorization flow. Tokens are stored only on the developer's own server and are never transmitted to third parties.
- **Public-facing post metadata** returned by the platform after a successful upload (post ID, post URL, publish status). This is used only to track which content has been published and to populate Whop creator-rewards submissions.

## What data we do NOT collect

- We do not collect, store, or process personal data from any user other than the developer / operator of the @stream_highlightshq accounts.
- We do not collect follower lists, comments, direct messages, or analytics for any account other than the operator's own.
- We do not sell, share, or transmit any data to third parties.

## Storage and retention

All credentials and operational data are stored locally on the developer's own infrastructure. OAuth tokens are retained until manually revoked by the developer through the platform's own developer dashboard. Operational records (post history) are retained for deduplication and may be deleted at any time by the developer.

## Third-party services used

The application interacts with the following platform APIs. Each platform's own privacy policy governs the use of data by that platform:

- TikTok Content Posting API — https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy
- Instagram Graph API / Meta — https://www.facebook.com/privacy/policy
- Twitch Helix API — https://www.twitch.tv/p/legal/privacy-notice
- YouTube Data API v3 — https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Your rights

Because this application processes data only on behalf of the single developer who owns it and the accounts it publishes to, no third-party user rights apply.
